Overview

PrintEase is a web-based platform that bridges users and local Xerox shops by digitizing the printing service process. It allows users to easily locate nearby shops, upload documents remotely, and make secure online payments. Designed for convenience, efficiency, and sustainability, PrintEase empowers both customers and shop owners while promoting eco-friendly practices by reducing unnecessary travel.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: Flutter (Dart)
  • Backend: Firebase Functions
  • Database: Firestore (NoSQL)
  • Hosting: Firebase Hosting
  • Payments: Razorpay / PhonePe Integration
